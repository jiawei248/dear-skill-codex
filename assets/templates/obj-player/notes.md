# Obj-Player Template Notes

A tall mobile-format card (420x840) rendered with p5.js Canvas. Bold grid-based layout with color-blocked sections — pink header, orange marquee, gray 3D wireframe grid, dark blue portrait zone, green and pink info blocks. Heavy Anton font with Space Mono monospace details.

It implements a `kinetic-collage` pattern: style-first brutalist composition with strong visual personality and interactive 3D element.

## Reference Use

Treat as reference. Codex may change the content to any subject that suits a bold, editorial card format — a person profile, a product showcase, a "character card" for the user. The core idea: a visually striking, brutalist card with interactive 3D element.

## What To Change Per Use

- document.title
- Header and marquee text
- Portrait/image content
- Product/detail info blocks
- Color block palette
- 3D grid animation parameters
- Typography content

## What Not To Copy Blindly

- the exact "NULL_OBJ" branding
- the specific pink/orange/green palette
- the vinyl record icon

## Best Fit

- fashion/streetwear themed gifts
- bold "character card" or "player profile" gifts
- design-enthusiast gifts
- edgy, experimental visual statements
- music/vinyl/culture themed gifts

## Watch Outs

- WebGL 3D grid requires GPU — may stutter on low-end mobile
- Anton font is VERY bold — use sparingly for readability
- brutalist aesthetic is polarizing — match to user taste
- dark/saturated colors may not render well on low-brightness screens
- do not use @latest or unstable external dependencies

## Delivery Note

Pinned CDN delivery for p5.js is acceptable for the final single-file HTML gift.
