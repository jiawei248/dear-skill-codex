# Cloud Text Rain Template Notes

A poetic auto-play scene where text characters orbit around a cloud shape, periodically fall as rain drops, and create ripple effects on a ground plane made of dense prose text. Uses a doorway/window frame composition.

It implements a `transformation` pattern: words become weather, language becomes landscape. The text literally rains down and pools into readable content.

## Reference Use

Treat as reference. Codex may change the cloud text, the ground prose, the color palette, frame shape, and rain behavior. The core idea: text transforms into atmospheric phenomena.

## What To Change Per Use

- document.title
- Cloud orbiting text (short, repeating phrase)
- Ground text (long prose, letter, or poem - needs ~1000+ chars to fill the grid)
- Wall/frame color
- Sky gradient colors
- Cloud colors (4-layer system)
- Rain drop interval
- Ripple parameters
- Door/window dimensions

## What Not To Copy Blindly

- the exact English love letter ground text
- the exact blue doorway palette
- the word "cloud" as orbiting text

## Best Fit

- poetic/literary moments
- transformation themes
- contemplative moods
- longer text that benefits from atmospheric presentation
- rain/water/weather metaphors
- gentle melancholy or wistful beauty

## Watch Outs

- needs substantial body text (~1000+ chars) to fill the ground grid convincingly
- auto-play only - no user interaction, which may feel passive
- the ground text is tiny and not meant to be fully read - it is texture
- the blue palette may not suit warm/energetic content
- do not use @latest or unstable external dependencies
