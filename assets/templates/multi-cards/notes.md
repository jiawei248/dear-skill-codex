# Multi-Cards Template Notes

Three side-by-side tall cards (phone-sized) on a near-black background. Left card is deep red with serif typography; center card is beige with perforated ticket edges and watermark stamp; right card is split gold/yellow with abstract SVG cutout art.

It implements a `kinetic-collage` pattern: style-first editorial composition with strong visual personality.

## Reference Use

Treat as reference. Codex may change the cards to represent chapters, memories, people, or any triptych concept. The core idea: multiple tall, beautifully designed cards side by side, each with distinct visual identity.

## What To Change Per Use

- document.title
- Number of cards (2-4)
- Card content, colors, and typography
- Ticket details and stamp text
- SVG art elements
- Background color

## What Not To Copy Blindly

- the exact hotel/travel document theme
- the exact red/beige/gold color scheme
- the specific SVG cutout shapes

## Best Fit

- luxury/sophisticated milestone gifts
- vintage-themed memory cards
- editorial-style gift presentations
- triptych storytelling (past/present/future)
- multi-faceted "portrait" of a person or relationship

## Watch Outs

- three tall cards need horizontal scroll on mobile — consider stacking
- Playfair Display is heavy — limit to headings
- SVG complexity affects load time
- perforated edges are CSS-heavy — test on mobile
- do not use @latest or unstable external dependencies

## Delivery Note

Single HTML file. Google Fonts CDN is acceptable.
