# Notebook Planner Template Notes

A two-page spread planner on warm paper background (#f4f1eb). Left page features a daily schedule timeline (08:00-18:00) with colored event blocks and a red "current time" indicator. Right page has priorities checklist and journal/notes section with ruled lines. Metal binder rings connect pages. Side tabs (JAN-MAY) with 3D page-flip animation on click.

It implements a `gifted-data-viz` / `memory-shelf` hybrid pattern: structured personal data presented as a warm, tangible artifact.

## Reference Use

Treat as reference. Codex may change the schedule to a relationship timeline, the checklist to achievements, or the journal to memories. The core idea: structured information in a warm, planner-like format that feels personal and handmade.

## What To Change Per Use

- document.title
- Schedule events and times
- Priority/checklist items
- Journal/notes text
- Month tab labels and count
- Paper and accent colors
- Date display

## What Not To Copy Blindly

- the exact 08:00-18:00 time range
- the specific month labels
- the exact pink/blue event colors

## Best Fit

- daily/weekly review gifts ("here's what your week looked like")
- achievement timelines
- structured memory compilations
- planning or goal-setting themed gifts
- "our schedule together" couple gifts

## Watch Outs

- two-page spread needs landscape or wide viewport — consider responsive stacking
- page-flip animation on tab click may confuse without hints
- schedule timeline is time-specific — ensure data makes sense
- metal ring effect uses box-shadow tricks — may render differently across browsers
- do not use @latest or unstable external dependencies
